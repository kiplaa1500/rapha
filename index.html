Why You Should Trust Us? Get Know About Us!

Rapha Physiotherapy and Naturopathy Hospital is a registered company operating in East Africa whose headquarters are in Kisumu, Tom Mboya Estate off Nyerere Rd, next to Golden Elites Academy, Kisumu Kenya.

The company is primarily involved in treating and training people in complimentary approaches to medicine. We combine both naturopathy (nature cure) and allopathy (modern medicine) in realizing the best outcomes in patient management We collaborate with Africa Inuka hospitals in Millimani Estate and in Town ( Odera street) whose facilities we use for laboratory and Radiology purposes.
We provide both inpatient and outpatient services from our facility in Tom Mboya estate.
